History & Origins

The Sama–Badjao are a seafaring Indigenous group from the Sulu Archipelago...

Identity and Language

Their identity is built on marine-based traditions. They speak Sinama.

Community

Traditionally organized in kin-based groups on houseboats or coastal stilt homes.

Leadership & Governance

The panglima, a respected elder, mediates conflicts and guides decisions.

Where They Live

  • Tawi-Tawi
  • Sulu
  • Basilan
  • Zamboanga
  • Borneo
  • Eastern Indonesia
